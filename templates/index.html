About ToolBox

Toolbox is an interactive platform for the learning of academic subjects. Our vision is to mitigate the level of education in the world and the middle east specifically through intuitive learning. We aim to help those who lack quality education by offering such a free of charge platform, we recognize that the lack of knowledge worldwide is an epideic deficiency, and see our solution to it as a viable method of solving this problem.
